Spelunky

Spelunky features 8-bit graphics displayed across four main worlds, each composed of 4 levels. The title also features the Spelunky staple of secret levels with the lucrative City of Gold.

World 1: Cave



The cave is the first world in Spelunky. It features simple enemies (hyperlink) like the snake, bat, and spider. The world's gold trap is a Indiana Jones-like bolder trap. Also present in the world are the first step in acessing hidden City of Gold.

World 2: Jungle



The jungle is the second world in Spelunky. It features the dangerous man-eaters, annoying monkeys, and explosive frogs. The world's gold trap is an idol above a disappearing platform. The world also features the secret Black Market, essential in accessing the hidden City of Gold.

World 3: Ice Caves



The ice caves are the third world in Spelunky. It features explosive UFOs and throwing yetis. The design of these levels are much more open than previous worlds. The world has a large Moai head than must be accessed to get to the hidden City of Gold.

World 4: Temple



The Temple is the final world in Spelunky. It features cavemen, jumping bird men, and quickly moving blocks. This world is extremly dangerous and extreme caution is recommended. The world has the door to the hidden City of Gold.
