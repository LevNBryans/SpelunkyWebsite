Spelunky 2 Secret World

Spelunky 2 features the most complex secret worlds of any Spelunky game.

  1. Get the Udjat Eye. Similar to Spelunky Classic or Spelunky HD, the Udjat eye can obtained from opening a locked box with a golden key in the first world.
  2. Get the special headgear. If you chose to go to Volcano for your second world, look for a large purple drill at the top of any of the levels. Slot the Udjat Eye into its receptacle, and the drill will mine downwards. Climb down the drill's chain and enter Vlad's Castle at the bottom of the level. The Crown can be found inside.
    If you chose to go to the Jungle for your second world, the Udjat Eye will alert you of the entrance to the Black Market. Blow up part of the level with a bomb, and enter the Black Market. The Hedjet can be bought or stolen from one of the shops.
  3. Get the Anhk. In Olmec's Chamber, have Olmec break the ground on the final floor and ride his head into the lava. Standing on his head allows you to access a special chamber where the Anhk can be found.
  4. Go to Abzu or the City of Gold. If you chose to go to Tide Pool, find Excaliber at the bottom of one of the levels. On the level with the lava floor, there will be a entrance to a area leading to under the lava. Pick up the idol (leave Excaliber at the top of the level) and be killed by the lava. After being revived, go through the door that was blocked by the lava to get to Abzu.
    If you chose to go to the Temple, kill Anubis for the Scepter. Find the golden door in one of the levels and enter the City of Gold.
  5. Get the Clay Tablet. If you went to Abzu, kill Kingu with Excaliber, granting you the Clay Tablet.
    If you went to the City of Gold, sacrifice yourself on the Kali Altar. When revived, you will be in Duat. Kill Osiris to get the Clay Tablet.
  6. Get Qilin. In the second level of Neo Babylon, there is a door to a large room with many pots. Bring the pot matching the description on the Clay Tablet's journal entry to the next level to get Qilin.
  7. Fly to the Sunken City. In the Tiamat boss room, fly upwards with Qilin to get to the rocket that takes you to the Sunken City.

If you want to go even further than the Sunken City...

  1. Get Hou Yi's bow. In the second world, there is a Tun shop that allows you to dig with a Mattock. Hou Yi's bow is hidden somewhere in the rocks accessible by mining.
  2. RECOMMENDED: Give Hou Yi's Bow to Waddler Instead of carrying Hou Yi's Bow, give it to Waddler in Olmec's chamber. On the far right of the first arena, there is one block in the floor that is not a stone brick. Behind this block is Waddler's chamber.
  3. RECOMMENDED: Get Hou Yi's Bow from Waddler In the Sunken Ocean, there is an extrance to Waddler's chamber where Hou Yi's bow will be IF you gave it to him. How nice of him!
  4. Get the Arrow of Light In the Sunken Ocean, there is a Tun shop. If you survive the enemy guantlet inside, you can get the Arrow of Light
  5. Fire the Arrow of Light into Hundun's eye After killing Hundun in the final level of the Sunken Ocean, use Hou Yi's Bow to Fire the Arrow of Light into Hundun's eye. This creates a portal that is the entrance to the Cosmic Ocean.

The Cosmic Ocean goes until 7-99 and it is brutal. I still have not beat it after more than 400 hours in the game. Good luck!
