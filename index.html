Spelunky

Spelunky is a roguelike platformer created in 2008 by Derek Yu. The gameplay varies somewhat by entry into the series, but all involve running and jumping, permadeath , and procedurally generated levels. The first entry into the series, Spelunky, rereleased as Spelunky Classic, defined the Spelunky formula. The next entry, Spelunky HD is a semi-remake of Spelunky Classic (as I will be referring to it from now on) was released in 2012. Spelunky HD brought mass appeal to Spelunky and vast treasures to Derek Yu and his company MossMouth The (as of now final) entry into the series is Spelunky 2, which modernized the series. The games have had vast critical and commercial success.
