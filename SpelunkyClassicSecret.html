Spelunky Classic/HD Secret World: City of Gold

Spelunky features a secret world: The city of gold. This world can be accessed by a series of steps.

  1. Get the Udjat Eye from the caves. The Udjat Eye is in a locked golden chest openable with a golden key. The golden chest and the golden key always appear in the same level.
  2. Get the Ankh from the Jungle. The Udjat Eye will blink and beep when you are close to the entrance to the Black Market. The entrance is covered with rock and a bomb is needed to uncover it. In the Black Market, the Ankh can be bought or stolen.
  3. Get the Hedjet from the Ice Caves. In one of the ice cave levels, there is a large Moai head. If the player has the Ankh and dies on that level, they will be revived inside the Moai head and receive the Hedjet.
  4. Go through the golden door in the Temple First, get the scepter from killing Anubis. Find the golden door and go through it.

You have now entered the City of Gold! In Spelunky HD there is still more to do.
  • Kill Anubis again and get the Book of the Dead.
  • There is a secret door accessable by riding Olmec's head into the lava. The Book of the Dead tells you where it is.
