Spelunky 2

Spelunky 2 is the most recent entry into the Spelunky series. It is much larger and more expansive than either of its predecessors. The game features all new mechanics, like mounts, backpacks, and alternate worlds. The game's secret route is also much more complex than that of the first two games.

World 1: Dwelling


The dwelling is the first world of Spelunky 2. It has a similar theme to the cave world of Spelunky Classic and Spelunky HD. The world ends with a boss fight with a large hedgehog.

World 2a: Volcana


Volcana is the one of the two available second worlds in Spelunky 2. It has volcano elements and firey enemies.

World 2b: Jungle


The Jungle is the one of the two available second worlds in Spelunky 2. It has jungle elements and poisonous enemies. This world is similar to the jungle world of Spelunky Classic and Spelunky HD.

World 3: Olmec's Chamber


Olmec's Chamber is a one level world consisting simply of a fight with Olmec. The chamber has three arena's above a pit of lava.

World 4a: Tidepool


The Tidepool is one of two available worlds after Olmec's Chamber. This world is ocean themed and has status effect enducing enemies.

World 4b: Temple


The Temple is one of two available worlds after Olmec's Chamber. This world is temple themed and has magical enemies.

World 5: Ice Caves


Unlike in Spelunky Classic and Spelunky HD, Spelunky 2's ice cave is one extremely tall level. The ice caves also have a boss fight with a Yeti and secret UFO.

World 6: Neo Babylon


Neo Babylon is the final non-secret world in Spelunky 2. This world is extremely dangerous and has many explosive enemies and insta-kill hazards. The final level of this world is a boss fight with Tiamet, which the winning of grants the easiest victory of the game.
